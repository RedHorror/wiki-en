Red Horror \(Red Horror: mafia; mafia\) is a team psychological step-by-step role-playing game with a detective storyline that simulates the struggle of members of an organized minority with an unorganized majority.



Residents of the city, exhausted from the binge of the mafia, make a decision to transplant all Mafia to prison. In response, the mafia declares war until the complete destruction of all decent citizens.
